Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions?

Find answers to the most commonly asked questions about MADHack 2024

Who can participate in MADHack?
MADHack is open to all undergraduate students from Sri Lankan universities. You can participate individually or form teams of up to 4 members. No prior mobile development experience is required!
What platforms can we develop for?
You can develop for any mobile platform including Android, iOS, or cross-platform solutions using frameworks like React Native, Flutter, or Xamarin. The choice of technology stack is entirely up to your team.
Is there a registration fee?
No! MADHack 2024 is completely free to participate. We believe in making technology accessible to everyone and removing financial barriers to innovation.
What should we bring to the event?
Bring your laptop, chargers, and any development tools you prefer. We'll provide WiFi, power outlets, meals, and refreshments throughout the event. Don't forget your student ID for verification!
How will projects be judged?
Projects will be evaluated based on innovation, technical implementation, user experience, market potential, and presentation quality. Our panel includes industry experts and academic professionals.
Will there be mentorship available?
Absolutely! We'll have experienced mentors from the industry and academia available throughout the event to help with technical challenges, design decisions, and project guidance.
